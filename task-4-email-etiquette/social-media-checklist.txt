SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DONT'S CHECKLIST 

DO'S
1.Think before posting online.
2.Protect your privacy and personal information.
3.Verify information before sharing.
4.Communicate respectfully with others.
5.Use social media for learning and professional networking.


DONT'S
1.Do not share fake news or rumours.
2.Do not post offensive or abusive comments.
3.Do not overshare personal details.
4.Do not post inappropriate content.
5.Do not spend too much time on social media.
