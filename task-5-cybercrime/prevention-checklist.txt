STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST
1. Never share OTP, password, or UPI PIN.
2. Do not click on unknown links.
3. Verify UPI collect requests carefully.
4. Use strong passwords.
5. Enable two-factor authentication.
6. Avoid public Wi-Fi for payments.
7. Keep apps and devices updated.
8. Log out from accounts on shared computers.


Report cybercrime at:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
