5. CYBERCRIME AWARENESS CASE STUDY (Task 5)
------------------------------------------------

Research was conducted on topics related to cybercrime, including phishing and payment fraud.

##Key points that were learned:
- Never share OTP, password, or UPI PIN.
- Do not click on unknown links.
- Verify UPI collect requests carefully.
- Use strong passwords.
- Enable two-factor authentication.
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for payments.
- Keep apps and devices updated.
- Log out from accounts on shared computers.


A checklist for preventing cybercrime was created.
